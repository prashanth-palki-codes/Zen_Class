Cricket 10

Game information

Each team gets 60 seconds to complete their innings in which 10 players from each team gets 6 balls to hit.

If a hit goes for score 0, then he will be considered as out and the batting passes to the next batsmen. Once the timer hits 0, the team-1 innings will be completed and batting of team-2 starts.

The team with high score will be considered as winners once the innings of both teams is completed and the user hits generate result
